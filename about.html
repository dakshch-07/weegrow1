Our Story

We noticed something extremely frustrating: local shops, restaurants, and clinic practitioners in Mumbai were being charged outrageous sums for slow, over-complicated websites that didn't bring in a single customer. We got tired of watching hard-working business owners get ripped off by agencies with hidden annual fees and domains they didn't even own.

Our clients kept asking us for simple, secure web solutions that actually work — and don't cost a fortune. So we built WeeGROW. No jargon, no hidden fees, just fast-loading, beautiful sites that make phones ring and drive customers directly to your shop.

Founder Q&A

Conversations With Our Founders

Jargon-free answers to the questions our clients ask us the most.

Q: What is a "WhatsApp Store" and why should I get one instead of a big e-commerce website?

Daksh: "Traditional e-commerce websites are slow to set up, complicated to manage, and cost a lot of money in transaction fees and security. A WhatsApp Store is incredibly simple. We build a sleek storefront menu where customers browse your products, tap what they want, and send a pre-filled order directly to your WhatsApp. There's no training needed—if you know how to chat, you know how to sell."

Q: A lot of agencies charge low setup fees but high yearly renewals. What is your policy?

Harshad: "We hate locked-in contracts and surprise bills. The price you see on our packages is a flat, one-time fee. No markups, no hidden maintenance, and no domain hostage-taking. We set up everything in your name, so you own your assets 100%. If you want support after the first year, it's completely optional and priced flat."

Q: Do I really need Google Business Profile (GMB) if I already have Instagram?

Harshad: "Instagram is great for branding, but when people in Mumbai want a dentist, a plumber, or a dinner spot *right now*, they go to Google and search 'dentist near me'. If you are not in Google Maps top 3 local results, you do not exist to them. We optimize your GMB profile so you capture high-intent customers exactly when they are ready to buy."
